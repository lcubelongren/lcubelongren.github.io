Dearest people,

I wanted to take a brief break from the usual 
newsletter format to send you a holiday card. 
The past few months for me have primarily been 
adjusting to a new style of living, a new job, 
new friends, all in a fascinating new place. 
Along with everything new, what stands out for 
me is how much I miss you all. Thank you for 
reading these little messages that I send out 
and for remaining a friend as I run off on 
this escape of mine.

I'll leave you with one of my favorite photos 
from my time here so far. Some background, we 
played a softball tournament between each of 
the departments on Christmas day. In the photo, 
I'm running to home plate. You can see in the 
background many of my newly made friends, 
coworkers, and roommates. It was a great time 
full of grilling, drinking, laughs, and cheers.




The farthest south softball tournament 
on the globe, 2022 (D. Rossi).

Wishing you all a happy holidays.

Luke
